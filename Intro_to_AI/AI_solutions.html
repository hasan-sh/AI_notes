intelligent agent: is a device that perceives its environment and takes actions that maximize its chance of successfully achieving its goals.

> A definition taken from a book we'll get in intelligent systems course.

2 types of ai:


1. weak ai; specific tasks where machines perform as good or better than humans do
   * game playing(i.e. chess or Go)
   * speech recognition
   * producing art
   * creative problem solving; giving all criteria of what needed for solving the problem;
2. strong ai; machines that _behave_ intelligently similar to humans; robots; equal in breadth and depth to humans.
